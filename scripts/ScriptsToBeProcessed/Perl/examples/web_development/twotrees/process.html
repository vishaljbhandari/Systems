We are very proud to show our olive oil-making process to the public and offer tours every Saturday, rain or shine, from 10 am to 3 pm. Remember to save room in your stomach for tasting our award-winning oils and breads.

To take our interactive tour, simply click on the numbered steps in the extra virgin olive oil making process. You will need the Flash Player installed in order to take our interactive tour.
